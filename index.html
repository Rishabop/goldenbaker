Follow My Journey

My Journey

From a child whisking batter to a baker chasing global dreams

🌱 The Beginning

Discovering My Passion

At four or five years old, my journey began in my mother's kitchen. Whisking batter, adding chocolate chips, and impatiently waiting for the oven timer—those nostalgic moments sparked a lifelong love for baking. That childhood excitement remains vivid and drives me every single day.

✨ The Turning Point

From Dreams to Reality

At thirteen, I transformed occasional baking into a daily adventure, launching my home bakery business. Recipe failures taught me resilience, while each success fueled my determination. What started as therapy became my truth—the profits were modest, but the experience was priceless and invaluable.

🎨 The Growing Years

Building My Brand

Through years of practice and refinement, I discovered the magic of social media marketing, creating engaging content that brought joy to customers and loved ones. Every creation reflects precision, artistry, and the love poured into every single bake. My academic journey sometimes suffered, but my drive to grow never wavered.

🌍 The Next Chapter

Switzerland Awaits

Since Grade Eleven, I've been convinced that Culinary Arts Academy Switzerland is where my potential truly belongs. I'm not just pursuing pastry mastery—I want to build a global bakery brand. The program's perfect fusion of culinary creativity and business education aligns perfectly with my vision. Proofing takes time, whether bread or business.

🚀 The Vision

Living the Dream

I live by my five P's: Passion, Perseverance, Patience, Purpose, and Profit. My fluency in German feels like destiny's gift, a puzzle piece falling perfectly into place. I'm ready to blend imagination with commerce, garnish my ideas with care, and serve them to the world with pride and excellence.
